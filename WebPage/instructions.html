Autobuyer Instructions

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 1

First go to the "downloads" section and download the newest version.
Unzip the file and start autobuyer.java. Login with your details. If you
haven't registered yet, go to the "account" section.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 2

Now you should see the "Account Overview". Click on one of the
gears to add your account details. Press save and you are ready to go.
Click on the check symbol, where you added your account details to
start the autobuyer.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 3

Wait until the the "EA-Login" is checked. Then go to your mail and
paste the security code of EA in the textfield. When the
"Verification Code" is checked wait until the "Security Answer"
is checked.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 4

Now you have the option between "BIN Sniping" and "Bidding",
select the one you would like to do. If you don't no the difference
you can find the explonation of the methods in the "Description"
section.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 5

Now you're in the main menu. In the top bar you can see the options
"Autobuyer", "Sniping Filter", "Trade Pile", "Settings" and "Stats".
In the "Autobuyer" section you see, which playerlist or filter is active,
the requests per second, the sleeping time an the players listed. If
you're happy with the settings, you can start the autobuyer with the
button "Start" or you can stop it with the button "Stop".

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 6

Obviously you can't start now because you don't have any players
in your playerlist or filter. Navigate to the "Sniping Filter" section.
Decide if you want to set up a playerlist or a filter. Enter all the
informations in the fields and then select one of the lists
(List 1, List 2, List3 or Filter 1, Filter 2, Filter 3). Finally click on save,
activate the list you want and you are finished.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Step 7

Now you're in the main menu. In the top bar you can see the options
"Autobuyer", "Sniping Filter", "Trade Pile", "Settings" and "Stats".
In the "Autobuyer" section you see, which playerlist or filter is active,
the requests per second, the sleeping time an the players listed. If
you're happy with the settings, you can start the autobuyer with the
button "Start" or you can stop it with the button "Stop".
